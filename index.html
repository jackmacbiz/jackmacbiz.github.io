About

JackMacBiz is a small digital-downloads studio. I design printable wall art, nursery prints, wedding stationery, t-shirt graphics, planner inserts, and stickers, and sell them as instant downloads to customers who want polished, easy-to-print files without the markup.

Every file is original work, designed for standard US Letter, A4, and common photo-frame sizes. Buyers get high-resolution PDFs and PNGs ready to print at home or send to a local print shop.
